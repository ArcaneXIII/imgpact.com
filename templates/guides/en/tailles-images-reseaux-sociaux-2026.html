Social Media Image Sizes 2026: Complete Reference

⏱ 6 {{ t.guide_min_read }} 📅 {{ t.guide_updated }}

Social media platforms crop, resize, and compress every image you upload. Using the wrong dimensions means your images look pixelated, important elements get cropped, or the platform's re-compression artifacts are visible. This guide lists the optimal image sizes for every major platform in 2026.

Instagram image sizes

FormatDimensionsAspect ratio
Square post1080 × 1080 px1:1
Portrait post1080 × 1350 px4:5
Landscape post1080 × 566 px1.91:1
Story / Reel cover1080 × 1920 px9:16
Profile picture320 × 320 px (displayed at 110 px)1:1

Instagram compresses JPEGs aggressively. To minimise re-compression artifacts, upload at exactly 1080 px width as a high-quality JPEG (85–90). Videos and Reels perform better at exactly 1080×1920.

Universal best practices

  • Format: JPEG for photos, PNG for graphics with text or transparency
  • Quality: export at JPEG 85–90 before uploading to minimise double-compression artifacts
  • File size limit: most platforms accept up to 8–30 MB but re-compress anything above ~500 KB
  • Colour space: use sRGB. CMYK profiles cause colour shifts on social platforms
Key takeaway: Always prepare images at the platform's recommended dimensions before uploading. Social platforms re-compress every image, uploading at exact dimensions with high JPEG quality (85–90) gives the best result after the platform's compression pass.
