YouTube Banner Size 2026: Channel Art Dimensions

⏱ 4 {{ t.guide_min_read }} 📅 {{ t.guide_updated }}

The YouTube channel banner (channel art) is one of the trickiest images to size correctly because it is displayed at different dimensions on every device. TV, desktop, tablet, and mobile all show different amounts of the image. Using the correct total canvas size and respecting the safe zone ensures your banner looks great everywhere.

The safe zone explained

YouTube scales your 2560×1440 image differently on each device:

  • TV: shows the entire 2560×1440 canvas
  • Desktop: shows the full width (2560 px) but only 423 px of height from the centre
  • Tablet: shows 1855 px wide × 423 px tall from the centre
  • Mobile: shows 1546 px wide × 423 px tall from the centre

The safe zone (1546×423 px, centred) is the only portion guaranteed to be visible on every device. Keep all essential content, channel name, tagline, logo, within this zone. Decorative background elements can extend to the full 2560×1440 canvas.

How to set up the safe zone in your design tool

  1. Create a canvas at 2560 × 1440 px
  2. Find the centre of the canvas: 1280 px horizontally, 720 px vertically
  3. Draw a guide rectangle 1546 px wide × 423 px tall, centred on the canvas:
    • Left edge: 1280 − 773 = 507 px
    • Right edge: 1280 + 773 = 2053 px
    • Top edge: 720 − 211.5 ≈ 508 px
    • Bottom edge: 720 + 211.5 ≈ 932 px
  4. Place all critical text and logos inside this rectangle
  5. Fill the rest of the canvas with your background design

YouTube displays the profile picture as a circle. Upload at 800×800 px with your logo centred and some margin to allow for the circular crop.

Video thumbnail

Thumbnails are one of the most important factors for YouTube click-through rate. Keep text large and legible at small sizes (thumbnails appear at ~168×94 px in search results) and ensure the image is compelling at a glance.

Design tip: Test your banner at 1546×423 px first, design it to look complete at that size. Then expand the canvas to 2560×1440 and add background elements that extend to the edges for TV display. This workflow ensures the safe zone is never neglected.
Key takeaway: Create your YouTube banner at 2560×1440 px but keep all essential text and logos within the 1546×423 px safe zone in the centre. Upload as JPEG or PNG under 6 MB. Upload your profile picture at 800×800 px, centred for circular display.
