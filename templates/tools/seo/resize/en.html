How to Resize an Image Online

  1. Upload your image (PNG, JPG, WebP, GIF, or BMP).
  2. Enter a new width or height in pixels, the other dimension updates automatically with aspect ratio locked.
  3. Or enter a percentage scale (e.g. 50% halves both dimensions).
  4. Choose a resampling filter: Lanczos3 for the best quality downscaling.
  5. Click Resize Image and download the result.

Resampling Filters Explained

  • Lanczos3: Sharpest result, recommended for photos and general use.
  • Bicubic: Smooth and natural, good for gradients and illustrations.
  • Bilinear: Fast with acceptable quality for most web images.
  • Nearest: Pixel-perfect for pixel art, icons, and crisp edges.

About the Resize Tool

imgpact's image resizer runs entirely in your browser via WebAssembly. Files are never uploaded, processing is instant and completely private. There is no maximum file size limit.
