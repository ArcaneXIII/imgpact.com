How to Convert a GIF to MP4

  1. Upload your animated GIF.
  2. Click Convert to MP4. FFmpeg.wasm processes the file entirely in your browser.
  3. Preview the resulting video once conversion finishes.
  4. Click Download MP4 to save the file.

Why Convert GIF to MP4?

  • Much smaller file size: A GIF converted to MP4 is typically 5–10× smaller with identical visual quality.
  • Better quality: MP4 supports millions of colors per frame vs GIF's 256-color limit.
  • Widely compatible: MP4 plays natively in all browsers, social media platforms, and video players.
  • Autoplay on social media: Twitter, Instagram, and Reddit display MP4 videos as looping silent animations.
