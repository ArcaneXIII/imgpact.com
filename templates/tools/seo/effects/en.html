How to Apply Effects to an Image

  1. Upload your image (PNG, JPG, WebP, or BMP).
  2. Click an effect card to select it, a preview appears immediately.
  3. Drag the Intensity slider to fine-tune the strength of the effect.
  4. Click Apply Effect to commit the effect to the image.
  5. Repeat with more effects to stack them, each apply adds on top of the last.
  6. Choose your output format and click Download.

Available Effects Explained

  • Grayscale: Converts the image to black and white.
  • Sepia: Adds a warm brownish tone, classic vintage photo effect.
  • Blur: Softens details. Useful for backgrounds or depth-of-field effects.
  • Sharpen: Enhances edges and fine details. Good for slightly blurry photos.
  • Brightness: Makes the image lighter or darker overall.
  • Contrast: Increases or decreases the difference between light and dark areas.
  • Invert: Flips all colors to their complement, creates a negative of the image.
  • Pixelate: Creates a mosaic / pixel art appearance.
