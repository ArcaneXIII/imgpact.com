How to Rotate or Flip an Image

  1. Upload your image (PNG, JPG, WebP, or BMP).
  2. Click any transform button, the preview updates instantly.
  3. Chain multiple transforms: rotate 90° left twice equals a 180° rotation.
  4. Select your output format (keep original, PNG, JPG, or WebP).
  5. Click Download to save the result.

Available Transforms

  • Rotate 90° Left: Turns the image counter-clockwise.
  • Rotate 90° Right: Turns the image clockwise, the most common fix for photos taken sideways.
  • Rotate 180°: Flips the image upside down.
  • Flip Horizontal: Creates a mirror image left-to-right.
  • Flip Vertical: Creates a reflection top-to-bottom.
