How to Convert a GIF to MOV

  1. Upload your animated GIF.
  2. Click Convert to MOV. FFmpeg.wasm handles the conversion entirely in your browser.
  3. Preview the resulting QuickTime video once conversion is complete.
  4. Click Download MOV to save the file.

When to Use MOV (QuickTime)

  • Apple ecosystem: MOV is Apple's native container, plays natively on macOS, iOS, Final Cut Pro, and iMovie.
  • Video editing: MOV files are widely accepted by professional video editors (Adobe Premiere, DaVinci Resolve, After Effects).
  • High-quality export: MOV supports high-bitrate video and lossless codecs for compositing workflows.

MOV vs MP4 vs WebM

  • MOV: Best for Apple devices and professional video editing workflows.
  • MP4: Best for maximum cross-platform compatibility.
  • WebM: Best for web embedding, smallest file size, royalty-free.
