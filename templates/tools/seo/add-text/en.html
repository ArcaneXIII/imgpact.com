How to Add Text to an Image

  1. Upload your image (PNG, JPG, WebP, or BMP).
  2. Type your text in the text input field.
  3. Choose your font size, color, and style options.
  4. Click on the canvas to position the text, or enter X/Y coordinates manually.
  5. Click Apply Text to render the overlay onto the image.
  6. Download the final image as PNG, JPG, or WebP.

Text Overlay Tips

  • Contrast matters: Use white or light-colored text on dark backgrounds and vice versa.
  • Multiple lines: Add text multiple times for different positions, sizes, or colors.
  • Watermarking: Add your name or URL at low opacity for a non-intrusive watermark.
  • Memes & captions: Use a large, bold font at the top or bottom, classic impact style.
