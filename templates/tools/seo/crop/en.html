How to Crop an Image Online

  1. Upload your image (PNG, JPG, WebP, GIF, or BMP).
  2. Choose a preset aspect ratio or use Free for any shape.
  3. Drag the crop handles on the canvas, or enter exact X, Y, Width and Height values.
  4. Select the output format and click Crop Image.
  5. Download the cropped result instantly.

Common Crop Aspect Ratios

  • 1:1, Instagram posts, profile pictures, avatars.
  • 16:9. YouTube thumbnails, widescreen website banners.
  • 4:3. Classic photography and presentation slides.
  • Custom, enter any W:H ratio for precise control.

About the Crop Tool

imgpact's crop tool processes images entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. No files are sent to any server, your images stay on your device and the tool works offline after the first load.
