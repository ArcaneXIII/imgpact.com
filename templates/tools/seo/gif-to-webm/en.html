How to Convert a GIF to WebM

  1. Upload your animated GIF.
  2. Click Convert to WebM. FFmpeg.wasm processes everything locally in your browser.
  3. Preview the resulting video once conversion is complete.
  4. Click Download WebM to save the file.

Why Convert GIF to WebM?

  • Best compression on the web: WebM with VP9 achieves even smaller files than MP4 H.264.
  • Full color range: WebM supports millions of colors per frame vs GIF's 256-color limit.
  • Open standard: WebM is a royalty-free format built into every modern browser.
  • Transparency support: VP9 in WebM supports an alpha channel for transparent backgrounds.
