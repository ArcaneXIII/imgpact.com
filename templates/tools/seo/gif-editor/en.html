How to Edit an Animated GIF

  1. Upload your animated GIF, all frames are extracted automatically.
  2. Select one or more frames in the frame panel to edit or rearrange them.
  3. Use the editing controls: reorder by dragging, delete unwanted frames, or adjust individual frame delays.
  4. Apply global adjustments: change overall speed, reverse the animation, or crop/rotate the entire GIF.
  5. Preview the edited result in real time before exporting.
  6. Click Export GIF and download the edited file.

What You Can Do with the GIF Editor

  • Reorder frames: Change the animation sequence by dragging frames to a new position.
  • Delete frames: Remove unnecessary frames to reduce file size.
  • Adjust timing: Set a per-frame delay in milliseconds to control speed.
  • Change speed: Speed up or slow down the whole animation globally.
  • Reverse: Play the GIF backwards with a single click.
  • Crop & Rotate: Fix composition and orientation.
